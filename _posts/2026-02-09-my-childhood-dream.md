When I was young (< 18 years old), my dream is to become a movie director. For the whole senior high school, I watched over 100 movies, escaped from schools and homework, dive myself in movie watching. I can't be more exciting to think about the movies I am gonna watch, everyday and every week.

Right now, my chilhood dream of working in the entertainment industry is dead. But the feeling of wanting to become a director of a film still stays the same as I work more in the academia and industry. When I prepare a talk, even a technical one, I am always thinking how I could make the talk more attractive, more dramatic, which can help people at least learn something after they left the talks. Just like my senior high school teacher mentioned, after high school you guys will forget all the things you learned but only remembered a few frames. I felt that today.
